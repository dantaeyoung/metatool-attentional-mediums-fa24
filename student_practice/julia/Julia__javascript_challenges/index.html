Metatool: Attentional Mediums is an experimental, experiential, cooperatively-run course on exploring how space and technology are mediums that shape our attention.
You are tasked with becoming a researcher
in an experimental class on space and attention.

The goal is to develop a hybrid between
an attentional technique and
a spatial & technological intervention
through practice and experience.
